📊 Customer Churn Analysis in Banking

Objective: Analyze customer behavior to understand why they leave a bank, and suggest strategies for customer retention using Python, Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

Business Context: Financial institutions lose significant revenue when credit card customers churn. With acquisition costs often 5-10x higher than retention costs, identifying at-risk customers and understanding churn patterns is critical for profitability.

🔍 Project Highlights

  • ✅ Data wrangling & cleaning in Python
  • ✅ Exploratory data analysis (EDA) with visual insights
  • ✅ SQL queries for churn analysis by segment
  • ✅ Dashboard created in Power BI for business presentation

📈 Key Insights

  • High credit utilization correlates strongly with churn
  • Low-income and young customers are more likely to leave
  • "Blue" cardholders have higher attrition than premium members
