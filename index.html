Objective: Analyze customer behavior to understand why they leave a bank, and suggest strategies for customer retention using Python, Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

Business Context: Financial institutions lose significant revenue when credit card customers churn. With acquisition costs often 5-10x higher than retention costs, identifying at-risk customers and understanding churn patterns is critical for profitability.
